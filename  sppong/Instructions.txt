This is a clone of the classic game PONG.
DirectX 9.0 is required to run the game

1) Press ESC to quit
2) Press F2 ro see FPS

Single Player
1) Left Paddle moves up with 'W' and down with 'S'

Two Player
1) Left Paddle moves up with 'W' and down with 'S'
2) Right Paddle moves up with 'Up arrow' and down with 'Down arrow'
